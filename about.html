Since 2017, Amelia has done more than read Tarot. She has refined her unique approach to an age-old divinatory art to become a catalyst for her clients’ self-discovery and growth. As a Mexican-American queer woman, Amelia brings a blend of cultural perspective, intuition, empathy, and her multi-disciplinary background as a writer and creative to her readings. She is passionate about helping individuals harness the transformative power of Tarot to navigate life’s twists and turns with clarity, courage, and compassion. Each reading is a collaborative journey, where we explore the currents of energy beneath the surface of life, illuminating the path forward toward a more fulfilling future.

Amelia is a Brooklyn-based Tarot reader, writer, and creative with a strong background in traditional esoteric practices. From a Mexican-American cultural background, Amelia grew up around Tarot, spiritual concepts, and folk magic. She is a Scorpio Sun, Leo Moon, and Virgo Rising, positioning her as a simultaneously analytic and empathic mind within an intuitive context. She lives in Williamsburg with her boyfriend and cat and is currently working on many creative projects, including a debut urban fantasy novel based on Mexican folklore.

Amelia offers in-depth, intuitive readings that are not only insightful and illuminating, but also empowering and affirming.Her mission is to make sure her clients do not just get answers to their questions, but also feel as though they have the tools and perspective they need to move forward onto a brighter path. Whether you are navigating a specific challenge, seeking guidance, or simply curious about what the future holds, Amelia is here to help.
